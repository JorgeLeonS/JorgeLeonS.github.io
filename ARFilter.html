VFS VAR Filter

What is it?

An Unreal Engine 5, AR marker based application, specifically deployed on Android using ARCore, where a variety of projects are displayed, it contains mostly 3D modeling work, ranging from high poly ZBrush texturized models, to rigging and animation. This, with the purpose of showing what people might can develop in the VAR Design and Development course at VFS.

How did we do it?

Along with Morlán Beltrán, we decided to use Unreal Engine 5 beacuse we wanted to experiment with the newest technology, and gain knowledge that would allow us to be more familiar with another project we developed together with other two class mates, Cyber Attack.
