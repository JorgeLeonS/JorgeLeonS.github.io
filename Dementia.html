Dementia

What is it?

Client project for the VR/AR design and development program at Vancouver Film School, wokring for TTT Studios.

How did we do it?

The team started with 4 people (me included), who did the all the development, later, some people from the same school joined and helped with design, story, and SFX.
It's an Unreal Engine 5 VR network game for up to four players.
